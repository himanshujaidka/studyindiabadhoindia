About us

Study India Badho India is India’s largest learning platform for Job Seekers, Professionals and individuals who are looking for Career Growth or Personal Development. Study India Badho India helps everyone to achieve their goals. It was founded by Mr. Paras Sachdeva and Mr. Lovkesh Sharma in 2018, with headquarters in Bengaluru, India. The company runs India’s best learning platform that provides personalized learning for students, homemakers, job seekers or personal enhancement. The company has 10 lac+ active students, who are taking advantage of its deep–structured content and powerful adaptive ideas. The company has experienced phenomenal top-line growth at a significant contribution margin, making it one of the few sustainable start-ups in the Indian ecosystem. The company has a sales office in 10+ cities.
